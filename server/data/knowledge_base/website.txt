# MT4 Trading Platform

MetaTrader 4 (MT4) is one of the world's most popular trading platforms. It offers advanced charting tools, technical analysis capabilities, and automated trading through Expert Advisors (EAs).

Key features include:
- Advanced charting with multiple timeframes
- Technical indicators and drawing tools
- Automated trading with Expert Advisors
- Real-time market data and news
- Mobile trading capabilities

MT4 is available for Windows, Mac, iOS, and Android devices.

---

# MetaTrader 5 Platform

MetaTrader 5 (MT5) is the next generation trading platform offering enhanced features over MT4. It provides more timeframes, technical indicators, and an economic calendar.

Key improvements:
- 21 timeframes vs 9 in MT4
- 38 technical indicators
- Built-in economic calendar
- Depth of Market (DOM)
- More order types

# Hantec Social

Hantec Social is a social trading platform that allows traders to follow and copy the trades of experienced investors. It facilitates collaborative trading and learning.

Features:
- Copy trading functionality
- Follow top traders
- Social trading community
- Performance tracking
- Risk management tools

Perfect for beginners who want to learn from experienced traders.

# InsightPro

InsightPro is Hantec's advanced market analysis tool. It provides real-time market insights, technical analysis, and trading signals to help traders make informed decisions.

Key capabilities:
- Real-time market analysis
- Technical indicators and signals
- Market sentiment analysis
- Economic calendar integration
- Custom alerts and notifications

Available on the Market Analysis page at hmarkets.com.

# Hantec Mobile App

Features:

# WebTrader

Hantec WebTrader allows you to trade directly from your browser without downloading any software. Access your trading account from any device with an internet connection.

Advantages:
- No download required
- Works on any device
- Automatic updates
- Secure browser-based trading
- Same features as desktop platform
