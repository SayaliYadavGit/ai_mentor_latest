What is a Forex VPS

A Forex VPS, or Virtual Private Server, acts as an instant bridge connecting your trading device to the wider trading network. It acts as a form of protection for traders against unforeseen circumstances that may disconnect them from the server.

This is especially valuable when a trader may be working from a home computer. If there was an error with the computer, a power outage or loss of internet connection for whatever reason, a trader may be left vulnerable. In the fast-moving FX markets, any small amount of time can be crucial, and a trader being unable to open or close positions for even a few minutes could have massive consequences. The Forex VPS keeps a trader permanently connected to the trading server, minimising the risk they undertake when trading.

Specifically, a VPS is a virtual machine that is hosted on a physical server. It operates independently, just like a dedicated server, but multiple VPS instances can run on a single physical server. Each VPS has its own resources, like CPU, RAM, and storage. Crucially, this ensures that your trading activities are not affected by the performance of other users on the same server.

Different VPS Options

There are many VPS solutions out there that give traders different options depending on their needs. A key aspect is the choice between managed and self-managed virtual private servers which will be compared below:

Managed VPS: Choosing a managed VPS means your hosting provider takes care of the initial virtual machine configuration. This option reduces your administrative burden but is usually more expensive. Traders should also contact the hosting provider to check if they support Forex trading on their managed VPS.
Self-Managed VPS: Self-managed VPS solutions are typically more cost-effective but require a higher degree of technical knowledge. Some companies offer pre-set configurations, but often the trader is responsible for having the correct setup and security of the server. This option grants traders greater control over the server's customization.
