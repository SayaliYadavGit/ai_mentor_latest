Our History

Hantec Markets is a subsidiary of the award-winning Hantec Group, a multi-national business group founded in 1990.



The Hantec brand is regulated across the world, including the United Kingdom, Australia, Hong Kong, and Mauritius, with over 30 years of group experience. The Hantec Markets brand was officially born in 2009, with our first office set up in London, United Kingdom. 



The launch of the London office signaled the beginning of a period of rapid growth, with our business footprint expanding to Latin America, Africa, South Asia and Southeast Asia, positioning us as one of the world’s most entrusted brokers.



At Hantec Markets, our purpose is to enhance the lives of our clients by creating and delivering an unrivalled trading experience. Our client-centric services are built on the pillars of reliable tools, responsive support, and the utmost security of funds.   

These form the cornerstone of our commitment to our clients across the world, combined with decades of industry expertise and a passion to build innovative products for traders across the world. Today, as a global, multi-regulated broker, Hantec Markets offers leveraged trading on global currencies, gold, equities, commodities, and more.
