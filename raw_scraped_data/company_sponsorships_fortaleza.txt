Hantec Markets partners with Fortaleza EC
Hantec Markets is proud to be the official trading partner of Fortaleza EC.
Championing a winning mindset

At Hantec Markets, we believe that success is built on more than just skill—it’s about passion, resilience, and the relentless pursuit of excellence. These values define us as a global leader in online trading, and they are the same principles that drive Fortaleza EC, one of Brazil’s most dynamic and passionate football teams.

Our commitment to these values have led us to partner with Fortaleza EC, uniting the worlds of trading and football, where precision, strategy, and unwavering dedication define success.

Through this partnership, we celebrate a shared philosophy between Hantec Markets and Fortaleza EC—one where preparation meets performance, and where passion fuels every move, whether on the pitch or in the financial markets.

By merging the energy of football with the dynamic world of trading, we are bringing fans and traders together in a new way, creating opportunities for both the trading and football communities to be a part unique sponsored experiences. Fans will be able participate in giveaways, matchday activations, VIP experiences and more.

Together with Fortaleza EC, we are championing the mindset that defines winners—in the markets and on the field.
