Not All Robots Are Created Equal: Common TypesÂ 

Thinking that every forex bot is the same is a common mistake. They come in various forms; each is designed for specific market conditions.Â 

Scalping Robots: These EAs are built for high-frequency trading. They enter and exit trades rapidly to capture tiny profits. They can trade multiple currency pairs but require a broker with ultra-low spreads and fast execution.Â 
Grid Trading EAs: A grid bot places a series of buy and sell orders at set intervals, aiming to profit from a ranging market. This strategy can be risky during a sudden price movement in one direction.Â 
News Trading Robots: These specialised EAs are designed to trade forex or even trade gold during high-impact news events, capitalising on volatility.Â
